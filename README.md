Hello! I'm Emanuele, an AI researcher based in Lausanne, currently pursuing an MSc in Neuro-AI at EPFL. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning and neuroscience, with a focus on self-supervised learning for physiological signals, biologically plausible architectures, and building representations that are robust to the variability inherent in real neural data. Before moving into Neuro-AI, I trained in Engineering Physics at Politecnico di Milano — a background that still shapes how I think about modeling and measurement.

### Research Interests

### Current Work

**AI Research Intern &middot; Logitech CTO Research Office**

Building spatially invariant foundation models for EEG. The core problem is constructing representations that are robust to the spatial and configurational variability of electrode placement — permutation, dropout, and montage shifts — through contrastive learning, masked modeling, and autoencoding pipelines with geometry-aware and conductivity-aware priors.

**Previously &middot; Idiap Research Institute**

Replaced the FastSpeech 2 decoder with AdLIF-based spiking neural network modules for biologically plausible text-to-speech synthesis. Studied dimensionality effects in SNN decoders and properties of biologically plausible speech generation.

### Selected Projects

**EEG Foundation Models**
Spatially invariant self-supervised learning for EEG with contrastive, masked, and autoencoding objectives. Geometry-aware and conductivity-aware architectural priors for robustness to sensor configuration variability.

**Bio-inspired TTS &middot; AdLIF Spiking Decoder**
FastSpeech 2 with an AdLIF-based spiking decoder. Exploration of biological plausibility and SNN dimensionality in speech synthesis, developed at Idiap Research Institute.

**fMRI Auditory Analysis**
Preprocessing, GLM, PCA, ICA, and K-means clustering on fMRI data from emotional auditory stimuli paradigms.

**TinyLLMs for Hate Speech Classification**
PEFT / LoRA fine-tuning on small language models with explainability analysis and comparison of adaptation strategies.

**LLM Educational Assistant**
Full fine-tuning pipeline: SFT, DPO, RAG, quantization, and reasoning-oriented prompting.
